## 二、投稿步骤（7 步）

### Step 1：注册 Springer Nature 账号
- 打开 `https://submission.springernature.com`
- 注册/登录 → 进入作者专区

### Step 2：开始新投稿
- 点击 "Submit manuscript"
- 选择 journal: `Empirical Research in Vocational Education and Training` (40461)

### Step 3：上传文件（按此顺序）

| # | 文件 | 说明 |
|:--:|------|------|
| 1 | `P5-ER-VET-正文-v1.2.docx` | 匿名正文（双盲，无作者信息） |
| 2 | `Title-Page.docx` | 独立标题页（作者/单位/ORCID/基金/伦理） |
| 3 | `Cover-Letter.docx` | 投稿信 |
| 4 | `Figure1-*.png` | 7维度条形图（grayscale） |

### Step 4：填写元数据
- **Title**: Technology-Assisted Self-Directed Professional Development for Cross-Disciplinary Vocational Teachers: A Mixed-Methods Study from Chinese UAV Education
- **Abstract**: （粘贴正文摘要）
- **Keywords**: vocational education and training; teacher professional development; cross-disciplinary teaching; self-directed learning; technology-assisted learning; mixed-methods; UAV education

## 三、Cover Letter 模板

```
Dear Editors,

We submit the manuscript titled "Technology-Assisted Self-Directed 
Professional Development for Cross-Disciplinary Vocational Teachers: 
A Mixed-Methods Study from Chinese UAV Education" for consideration 
in Empirical Research in Vocational Education and Training.

This study addresses the structural phenomenon of cross-disciplinary 
teaching assignments in vocational education—a challenge that has 
intensified globally with rapid VET programme expansion. We report 
a mixed-methods investigation of the NFSC+Agent self-directed 
professional development model, validated through multi-stakeholder 
data from 10 semesters of teaching practice, peer teacher evaluations 
from 30+ institutions, and student usability assessments.

The manuscript makes three contributions aligned with ER-VET's scope:
(1) empirical validation of a zero-cost technology-assisted teacher 
development model for resource-constrained VET settings; (2) a 
multi-stakeholder evaluation framework combining peer review and 
standardised usability instruments; and (3) identification of 
cross-disciplinary transfer conditions with implications for VET 
policy and practice.

This work is original, has not been published elsewhere, and is not 
under consideration by any other journal. All authors have approved 
the manuscript for submission and declare no competing interests.

We believe this manuscript will be of interest to ER-VET's 
international readership of VET researchers, policymakers, and 
teacher educators.

Respectfully,
[Corresponding Author Name]
[Affiliation]
[Email]
```
