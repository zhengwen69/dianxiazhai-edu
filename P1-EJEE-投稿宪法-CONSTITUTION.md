## Chapter 1: Format Rules

### Rule 1 (Length)
**6,000-8,000 words** (excluding references, tables, figures). Grade: **CRITICAL**.

### Rule 2 (Abstract)
**150-250 words**, structured (Purpose/Method/Findings/Conclusions). Grade: **CRITICAL**.

### Rule 3 (Keywords)
**4-6 keywords**. Grade: **WARNING**.

### Rule 4 (References)
**APA 7th Edition** format. **≥25 references**, ≥60% from international (English) sources. Sequential numbering. Grade: **CRITICAL**.

### Rule 5 (Structure)
IMRaD format: **Introduction → Theoretical Framework → Method → Results → Discussion → Conclusion**. Grade: **CRITICAL**.

### Rule 6 (Self-citation)
Self-citation ratio **<10%**. Grade: **CRITICAL**.

### Rule 7 (Figures/Tables)
Embedded in text. High-resolution (≥300 dpi). Clear captions. Grade: **WARNING**.

## Chapter 2: Quality Control

### Rule 8 (Version Safety)
Increment version before any modification. Grade: **CRITICAL**.

### Rule 9 (AI Trace Removal)
Scan and remove AI fingerprints: "It is worth noting", "Moreover", "In addition", "Furthermore", "This paper aims to", passive overuse. Grade: **CRITICAL**.

### Rule 10 (Reference Verification)
Verify all references via DOI/CrossRef. Remove unverifiable entries. Grade: **CRITICAL**.
