## 八、Cover Letter 模板要点

```
Dear Editor,

We submit "The NFSC Pedagogy: Narrative-Driven Cognitive Progression in 
Graduate Engineering Education" for consideration in EJEE.

This paper presents a four-dimensional progressive teaching model that 
explicitly sequences narrative engagement before analytical framework 
introduction—addressing the motivational-cognitive gap identified in recent 
EJEE scholarship (Edström & Kolmos, 2014; Lönngren et al., 2020).

The manuscript:
- Reports a practitioner case study across three teaching cycles (N≈90)
- Provides thematic analysis with Cohen's κ = .74 intercoder reliability
- Engages 44 references including 6 EJEE publications
- Has not been submitted elsewhere

We believe this work contributes to EJEE's tradition of publishing practitioner 
scholarship in engineering education.

Sincerely,
HUANG Zhengwen (on behalf of all authors)
```
