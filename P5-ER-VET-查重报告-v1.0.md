## 一、P5 vs P1 (NFSC-EJEE)

### 1.1 >60% 句子重叠的段落

**无直接 >60% 句子重叠。** P1 和 P5 使用不同的研究语境、不同的文本组织方式，且没有发现逐句重复的段落。P1 描述 NFSC 作为固废课堂八周教学法；P5 描述 NFSC 作为跨学科教师自学协议。两者的 NFSC 四阶段概念一致，但表述方式不同。

具体对比：
- P1 将 NFSC 以周次展开（Narrative Week 1, Framework Week 2, Scenario Weeks 3–7, Creation Week 8），配有具体场景类型（EIA debate, Nudge design, mock environmental court）

**无重叠。** P1 不涉及 Teacher Z。P1 的研究对象是固废课程研究生（~30人/周期），不是跨学科高职教师。

### 1.3 NFSC 方法论描述重叠

**概念层面重叠（非文本层面）**：

## 二、P5 vs P2 (KJSS 双门户)

### 2.1 >60% 句子重叠的段落

**无直接 >60% 句子重叠。** 但存在以下接近重叠的段落：

**SUS 描述（中度重叠 ~40-50% 语义相似）**：

P5 §2.4: "The System Usability Scale (SUS), developed by Brooke (1996), has become one of the most widely used standardised instruments for evaluating the usability of technology systems. Its ten-item, five-point Likert format yields a composite score on a 0–100 scale, with established benchmarks: scores above 68 are considered above average, and scores above 80.3 are in the 'excellent' range (Sauro & Lewis, 2016)."

P2 §2.4: "The System Usability Scale (SUS; Brooke, 1996) provides a standardised, ten-item instrument for measuring subjective usability. SUS yields a single composite score on a 0–100 scale, with scores above 68 considered 'above average' and scores above 80.3 considered 'excellent' (Bangor et al., 2009)."

**分析**：两段均描述 SUS 的 0-100 分制和 68/80.3 基准值，但由于这是 Brooke (1996) 标准化工具的通用描述，此类重叠属于正常学术写作（任何使用 SUS 的论文都会描述相同基准值）。P5 引用 Sauro & Lewis (2016)，P2 引用 Bangor et al. (2009)，说明两文独立选择了不同的基准文献来源。

**TAM 描述（低度重叠 ~30% 语义相似）**：

P5 §2.4: "The Technology Acceptance Model (TAM), originating from Davis (1989), measures two core constructs—perceived usefulness (PU) and perceived ease of use (PEOU)—that predict user adoption of information technology."

P2 §2.4: "the Technology Acceptance Model (TAM; Davis, 1989) posits that users' behavioural intention to adopt a technology is determined by two cognitive constructs: perceived usefulness (PU)... and perceived ease of use (PEOU)..."

**分析**：同为 Davis (1989) 的标准理论描述，语义结构相似，但措辞不同。P5 简短（1 句），P2 展开为完整段落。

**SUS 计分公式**：两文均给出相同公式（奇数项 raw-1，偶数项 5-raw，总分×2.5），这是 Brooke (1996) 的标准化公式，任何 SUS 研究都会这样写。

## 四、P5 vs P4 (SAJE — Teacher Z)

### ⚠️ **这是五对比中重叠最显著的一对。** P4 和 P5 均以 Teacher Z 为中心案例，均描述 NFSC+Agent 自学模型在 UAV 跨学科教学中的应用。

### 4.1 >60% 句子重叠的段落

**没有逐字逐句 >60% 重叠的段落。** 两文以不同的英文表达描述相同的事实和概念，但以下是语义高度相似的段落：

#### 重叠段落 1：NFSC 四阶段定义（~50% 语义重叠）

**P5 §3.2**：
> "First, the NFSC methodology provided a structured four-phase self-study protocol: Narrative (motivation triggering through authentic problem scenarios), Framework (systematic knowledge mapping across the target discipline), Scene (competence cultivation through situated practice), and Creation (production of usable teaching outputs)."

**P4 §5.1**：
> "Its four components—Narrative (a coherent professional story), Framework (systematic knowledge mapping), Scene (contextualised scenario-based learning), and Creation (tangible outputs)—functioned as a self-study protocol substituting for the curriculum structure of formal training."

**分析**：两段均为对 NFSC 四阶段的解释性定义，分别给出四个阶段的简短括号说明。说明措辞不同（P5: "motivation triggering" / P4: "coherent professional story"），但描述的是同一概念框架。这不是文本抄袭，而是同一概念在目标期刊不同语境下的独立重写。

#### 重叠段落 2：三种跨学科迁移条件（~55% 语义重叠，**最接近实质重叠**）

**P5 §5.3**：
> "First, an **anchoring knowledge domain** is necessary: the teacher's existing disciplinary expertise must provide a cognitive bridge to the target domain. Teacher Z's background in surveying—with its spatial reasoning, coordinate systems, and field data collection methods—offered conceptual anchors for UAV flight planning, aerial photogrammetry, and remote sensing. [...] Second, a **structured self-study protocol** is essential. The NFSC four-phase sequence—Narrative, Framework, Scene, Creation—converts the amorphous challenge of 'learning UAV technology' into a manageable sequence of cognitive operations [...] Third, **institutional tolerance** for self-directed experimentation is a precondition."

**P4 §5.2**：
> "**An anchoring knowledge domain.** Every teacher brought a pre-existing knowledge structure—land management and environmental engineering (Teacher Z), solid waste engineering (Teachers S and W), engineering problem-solving (Teacher H)—that supplied conceptual frameworks onto which new knowledge could be mapped. [...] **A structured self-study protocol.** The NFSC methodology provided a replicable cognitive sequence: narrate, map, simulate, produce. [...] **Institutional permission for self-directed experimentation.** All four teachers operated in environments that allowed—or at least did not obstruct—self-directed learning."

**分析**：这三条迁移条件在两文中是**相同的分析框架**（相同的三个条件、相同的顺序、相同的核心论点），但表述方式和细节不同：
- P5 以 Teacher Z 单一案例展开每个条件的解释
- P4 以四个案例（Teacher Z + Teachers S, W, H）展开每个条件的解释
- P5 使用 "institutional tolerance"，P4 使用 "institutional permission for self-directed experimentation"，用词不完全一致但概念相同
- P5 对 Teacher Z 的调查背景给出具体技术细节（spatial reasoning, coordinate systems），P4 只概括为 "land management and environmental engineering"

**这是同一研究发现（迁移条件）在不同目标期刊中的独立表述，属于同源数据的不同呈现，但应引起注意——两文传递了相同的研究结论。**

#### 重叠段落 3：Teacher Z 的跨学科背景描述（~40% 语义重叠）

**P5 §3.2**：
> "Teacher Z—transitioned from a surveying and environmental science background to full UAV teaching competence. Teacher Z completed three teaching reform research projects and obtained a copyright registration during this period."

**P4 §1**：
> "Teacher Z, an instructor at [University A] in China, holds qualifications in land resource management (BSc) and environmental engineering (MSc) but no formal UAV training. Over the four years from 2021 to 2025... he built a complete UAV programme (10 courses), won three funded research projects, and registered intellectual property for his teaching system."

**P5 §5.1**：
> "Teacher Z—whose original disciplinary grounding was in surveying and environmental science—developed a complete eight-course UAV curriculum spanning four categories... completed three teaching reform research projects, and obtained a copyright registration."

**分析**：两文描述同一人物的相同事实（背景、年限、课程数量、课题数量、版权），使用了不同的措辞。P4 给出更具体的学位信息 (BSc land resource management, MSc environmental engineering)，P5 使用较概括的描述 (surveying and environmental science background)。课程数有差异：P5 说 8 门，P4 说 10 门——说明两文在不同时间点撰写或计数方式不同。

- 同一时间跨度（2021–2025）
- 同一干预模型（NFSC+Agent）
- 同一产出（3 课题 + 版权 + 10/8 门课程）
- 同一零成本定位

**差异化处理**：
- P5 将 Teacher Z 置于更广的混合方法框架中（三个数据模块、多利益相关方评估），定位为"ER-VET 实证研究"
- P4 将 Teacher Z 置于更细的传记式纵向案例中，定位为"SAJE 面向发展中国家的政策启示研究"
- P5 强调 VET 教师专业发展、工具验证、可迁移性；P4 强调 BRICS/南南合作、南非 TVET 政策
- 两文在结论部分使用了非常相似的 "pragmatic bridge" 修辞

### 4.7 关键发现：共享分析框架和结论

**三个迁移条件**（anchoring knowledge domain, structured self-study protocol, institutional tolerance/permission）在两文中作为核心研究发现出现。虽然表述方式不完全相同，但传递了相同的分析论点。这是 P5-P4 之间最需要注意的概念重叠。

**"Pragmatic bridge" 修辞**：
- P5 §5.4: "The NFSC+Agent approach is a pragmatic bridge for VET teachers"
- P5 §6: "it offers a pragmatic bridge: a structured, zero-cost pathway"
- P4 Abstract: "The model is a bridge: it does not replace formal teacher education"
- P4 §6: "The model is a bridge—a pragmatic tool for contexts where formal training is out of reach"

这是同一作者对同一模型在不同论文中使用了相同的修辞框架。

### 4.8 结论

P5 与 P4 之间存在**事实层面和概念层面的显著重叠**，因为两文以同一案例（Teacher Z）为核心，使用同一干预模型（NFSC+Agent），并得出类似的研究结论（三个迁移条件）。但两文在以下方面有实质性区分：

1. **研究设计不同**：P5 是混合方法多利益相关方实证研究（三个数据模块，N≥120），P4 是纵向单一案研究（N=1+3 辅助案例）
2. **理论锚定不同**：P5 以 ER-VET 文献为锚点（Bottling et al., Pargmann et al., Gentner et al.），P4 以 SAJE 文献为锚点（Verster et al., Naidoo, Chuene & Teane, Sepadi & Themane）
3. **读者/政策指向不同**：P5 面向 VET 教师教育研究者（关注工具验证、多利益相关方评估），P4 面向发展中国家教育政策制定者（关注 BRICS、南南合作、零成本可迁移性）
4. **数据呈现不同**：P5 的数据尚未收集（[DATA PENDING]），P4 呈现了完整的纵向数据
5. **文本层面**：**没有发现逐句复制或 >60% 句子重叠的段落**

**建议**：两文应互相引用以明确关系。P5 可在 P4 出版后将其引为 "single-case companion study"；P4 可在 P5 出版后将其引为 "multi-stakeholder extension study"。如果在同一时间投稿，COVER LETTER 中应声明两文的关系以符合双盲审稿伦理。
